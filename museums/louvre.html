Louvre Museum

Discover world-renowned masterpieces from the Louvre's collection.

Mona Lisa

Leonardo da Vinci, c. 1503–1506
Perhaps the most famous artwork in the world, the Mona Lisa captivates with her enigmatic smile.

Liberty Leading the People

Eugène Delacroix, 1830
A symbol of freedom and revolution in France, this painting commemorates the July Revolution of 1830.

Venus de Milo

Ancient Greek Sculpture, c. 130–100 BC
This iconic statue represents Aphrodite, the Greek goddess of love and beauty.

Winged Victory of Samothrace

Ancient Greek Sculpture, c. 200–190 BC
This dramatic sculpture celebrates the goddess Nike and is renowned for its sense of movement.

La Grande Odalisque

Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, 1814
A masterpiece of Orientalism, this painting is admired for its elegance and sensuality.

The Raft of the Medusa

Théodore Géricault, 1819
A monumental painting depicting the aftermath of a contemporary French shipwreck, symbol of Romanticism.
