The British Museum

Explore some of the most iconic treasures of the British Museum, London.

Rosetta Stone

Discovered in 1799, the Rosetta Stone unlocked the secrets of Egyptian hieroglyphs and is one of the museum's most famous pieces.

Parthenon Sculptures (Elgin Marbles)

Ancient Greek marble sculptures from the Parthenon, Athens, renowned for their historic and artistic significance.

Assyrian Lamassu

Majestic winged bulls with human heads that guarded ancient Assyrian palaces, symbolizing protection and power.

Standard of Ur

A remarkable Sumerian artifact from ancient Mesopotamia, depicting scenes of war and peace in intricate mosaic.

Sutton Hoo Helmet

An Anglo-Saxon ceremonial helmet found at Sutton Hoo, Suffolk, and a symbol of early English history.

Lewis Chessmen

12th-century chess pieces carved from walrus ivory, offering a fascinating glimpse into medieval life and culture.
