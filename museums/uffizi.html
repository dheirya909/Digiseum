The Uffizi Gallery

Discover masterpieces of the Italian Renaissance in Florence's celebrated Uffizi Gallery.

The Birth of Venus

Sandro Botticelli, c. 1484–1486
One of the most iconic paintings of the Renaissance, depicting Venus emerging from the sea.

Primavera

Sandro Botticelli, c. 1482
An allegory of spring filled with mythological figures and intricate symbolism.

Annunciation

Leonardo da Vinci, c. 1472–1475
A remarkable early work by Leonardo, notable for its detail and light.

Medusa

Caravaggio, c. 1597
A dramatic depiction of the mythological Gorgon, painted on a ceremonial shield.

Doni Tondo

Michelangelo, c. 1506–1508
The artist's only finished panel painting, featuring the Holy Family.
